Privacy Policy

At LONERGRAM, we take privacy seriously. In fact, we care so much about your privacy that we don't store ANY of your information. We don't see your pictures. We don't read your posts. We don't even know who we are. We don't care. Even if you export your content and send it to us in a file called "pleaseRead.json", we won't. That's how much we care.
